The TCP is a brand new boss entity added to your world. He has is very powerful as well as intelligent and has four phases, with each drastically increasing his power. He is also immune to fall damage and knockback and has an incredible 3 block reach.

Summoning the TCP:

Simply craft and use the "Infamous Wool" item

![image](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1601769/description_281dae9f-956a-4c62-afbf-bced794447cd.png)

Phases:

Phase 1: Base form! Slow attack speed

Phase 2: TCP gains 2x attack speed

Phase 3: TCP begins to crit-attack you, dealing 1.5x damage

Final Form: TCP gains +1 blocks of reach and begins to sprint, even while crit attacking. This attack may be quite difficult to defend

Building:

If the TCP is unable to reach you, he will use wool to bridge towards you

![image](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1601769/description_d45083f4-61ef-45bd-9a40-9290d39c7a63.png)

This also happens whenever the entity gets stuck or needs to cross a hazard such as water or lava

![image](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1601769/description_6316f3fc-564e-4c9f-8430-2de70463c4e3.png)

In certain cases, he will choose to tower up to you

![image](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1601769/description_cb88de7b-7bf9-4e06-bb63-357c7c2ba503.png)

Depending on his position from you, he will instead decide to staircase towards you

![image](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1601769/description_153ae94a-78ec-44fc-b851-7f76cf1cd45d.png)

Rewards: The TCP drops 2-3 "Essence of PVP" Using three of these items and a few other ingredients, you can craft the TCP mask.
